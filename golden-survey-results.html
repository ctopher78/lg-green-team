Recommended Rollout Tiers

Tier 1 — Start Now

High interest • Low-medium cost • Simple compliance
Talks / Events / Clubs Outdoor Skills Repair Clinics Sewing / Fiber Arts

Talks and events provide foundational infrastructure every other activity shares — chairs, A/V, tables. Outdoor skills are nearly free to launch with strong, consistent community interest. Repair clinics and sewing both have solid demand, low compliance burden, and grow well incrementally.

Tier 2 — Plan & Fund

Strong passion signal • Medium investment • Manageable compliance
3D Printing / Design Painting / Printmaking Woodworking

3D Printing ranks #1 in top priorities despite modest overall interest — enthusiasts are highly motivated. Painting has a strong passion-to-cost ratio; watch supply burn. Woodworking has consistent demand and manageable buildout with the right safety investment (SawStop is non-negotiable).

Tier 3 — Long Game

High cost or compliance • Or weaker demand signal
Community Kitchen Ceramics Metalworking / Welding Small Business Dev

Community Kitchen is the most desired activity but carries the highest regulatory and capital bar — health dept certification, licensed HVAC/fire contractor, NSF equipment. Ceramics shows many curious but few committed (only 5 top-priority picks). Metalworking has the lowest passion signal and highest ongoing safety burden; revisit after woodshop is established. Small Business Dev absorbs into the events space.
